Meet The Team

Drew started his filmmaking jouring in back in 2021. Initially his started off making fun shorts for his church which eventually caught the eye of a local prodigy wedding filmmaker. He began second shooting for other filmmakers specializing in luxury western weddings. Drew learned fast and developed his own uniuqe style. Love Story Lens was founded upon Drew's passion for capturing the best day of his couple's lives. Drew's skill at seeing beyond his camera allows him to preserve memories that last forever.

"Drew has been an amazing person to work with. He made us feel comfortable and excited for our big day, and helped make the day amazing and memorable."

- Joshua and Cheyenne
